About

Stephanie Bradbury is a digital and interactive marketing whiz, an organizing pro, enjoys spearheading projects from beginning to end, and has an undying passion for all things entertainment and sports.

Stephanie has gained experience growing a presence for her clients through email marketing. She has worked with an array of companies including Universal Pictures, Cadillac, Madison Square Garden, Radio City Music Hall, and both Broadway and off-Broadway productions. Stephanie's experience also reaches the start-up world where she thrived in creative and innovative partnerships and business development while managing clients ranging from local governments to Fortune 500 Companies.

Outside of the office, Stephanie is a devoted volunteer and trained team leader with New York Cares, is always in search of live music, and is an avid baseball fan. With a deep passion for learning, Stephanie furthers her knowledge of digital and interactive marketing and technology through continuing education courses at NYU and General Assembly.
